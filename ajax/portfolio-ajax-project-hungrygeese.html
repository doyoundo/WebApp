Hungry Geese Competition

Project Info:

Hungry Geese from Kaggle is a multi-play simulation. The rule is to train an AI agent to play against other "geese" and survive the longest. The agent needs to keep searching for food while not running into other agents. I trained the geese with many deep reinforcement algorithms and different neural network parameters to obtain the highest score.
