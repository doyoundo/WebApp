Multi-Bone IK

Project Info:

Multi-Bone IK is an user-friendly system to simplify a very long process of development of animations in game, movie, and any graphics field. The system is developed in UE version. Targets include bipedal, quadpedal, eight-legged (spider), and no legged (snake). Furthermore, IK not only applies to feet, but also to head, neck, spine, elbow, knee, hand/feetm and even to fingers.

Project Details:

  • Technologies:C++, Unreal Engine, Python
  • Date:Jan. 2021 - May 2021
  • Reference: Dragon IK
